## About this course

This course empowers academic faculty, instructional designers, and others to teach and assess AI Fluency in instructor-led settings.

At Anthropic, we believe that empowering people with AI requires engaging with a wide range of human perspectives and experiences. This course empowers participants to apply the 4D Framework to their own educational practice. Participants will learn to apply the 4D Framework to their own teaching practice, using AI as a thinking partner to enhance course design, create coherent learning materials, and develop authentic assessments while modeling responsible AI collaboration for their students.

This course is the result of a long partnership between Anthropic and professors Rick Dakan and Joseph Feller. It builds on their experiences in training fellow educators and teaching the AI Fluency Framework to their students, and on feedback and questions that arose from the earlier courses.

The work was supported in part by the Higher Education Authority (Ireland) through the National Forum for the Enhancement of Teaching and Learning.

## Key Concepts for Teaching AI Fluency

### The 4D Framework in Teaching

1. **Delegation** - Teaching students when to delegate tasks to AI (and when not to)
2. **Description** - Helping students craft effective prompts for learning
3. **Discernment** - Teaching students to critically evaluate AI outputs
4. **Diligence** - Modeling and assessing responsible AI use

### Teaching Strategies

- **Scaffolding** - Building AI Fluency progressively
- **Modeling** - Demonstrating responsible AI collaboration
- **Assessment** - Designing authentic assessments that develop AI Fluency
- **Integration** - Incorporating AI across the curriculum thoughtfully

### Applications in Instruction

- **Course Design** - Use AI to enhance course structure and materials
- **Learning Materials** - Create coherent, high-quality materials with AI assistance
- **Assessments** - Develop assessments that evaluate both AI Fluency and disciplinary expertise
- **Institutional Change** - Build capacity for responsible AI integration

## Course Philosophy

**Teaching AI Fluency is not about**:
- Banning AI tools
- Uncritically embracing AI
- Teaching prompt engineering tricks

**Teaching AI Fluency is about**:
- Developing lasting competencies for responsible AI collaboration
- Using the 4D Framework to structure AI integration
- Preparing students for a future where AI Fluency is essential
- Maintaining human agency and critical thinking
