## About this course

This course empowers nonprofit professionals to develop AI fluency in order to increase organizational impact and efficiency while staying true to their mission and values.

At Anthropic, we believe that empowering people with AI requires engaging with a wide range of human perspectives and experiences. Nonprofit professionals are uniquely positioned to leverage AI for social good—but only if they can approach it intentionally, with resilience and clear purpose.

This course helps nonprofit staff—whether in fundraising, communications, program delivery, operations, or leadership—build practical AI collaboration skills through the 4D Framework (Delegation, Description, Discernment, and Diligence). In this course, we'll explore how the framework applies to common non-profit tasks, and consider what it means to implement AI across a nonprofit organization.

This course is the result of a partnership between Anthropic and GivingTuesday, drawing on research with nonprofit professionals about their actual needs, concerns, and aspirations for AI adoption.

The work builds on the *AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations* course, adapted specifically for the nonprofit context where limited resources, multiple stakeholder accountabilities, and mission-driven work create unique considerations for AI collaboration.

## Key Concepts for Nonprofits

### The 4D Framework in Nonprofit Context

1. **Delegation** - Knowing when to delegate tasks to AI (while maintaining mission alignment)
2. **Description** - Crafting effective prompts for nonprofit communications and operations
3. **Discernment** - Critically evaluating AI outputs for accuracy, ethics, and mission alignment
4. **Diligence** - Responsible and ethical AI use in nonprofit contexts

### Applications in Nonprofit Work

- **Fundraising** - Use AI to enhance donor communications and grant writing
- **Communications** - Create compelling content while maintaining authentic voice
- **Program Delivery** - Streamline operations and improve service delivery
- **Data Analysis** - Understand your impact and optimize resource allocation
- **Workflow Augmentation** - Integrate AI thoughtfully into existing processes

### Unique Considerations for Nonprofits

- **Limited Resources** - Doing more with less using AI tools
- **Multiple Stakeholder Accountabilities** - Maintaining trust with donors, beneficiaries, and boards
- **Mission-Driven Work** - Ensuring AI use aligns with organizational values
- **Privacy & Data** - Protecting sensitive beneficiary and donor information
